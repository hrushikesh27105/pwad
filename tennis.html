Tennis Rules

  • Game starts with a serve diagonally across court.
  • Ball must land inside opponent’s boundary.
  • Points system: 15, 30, 40, Game.
  • Matches are best of 3 or 5 sets.
  • Faults include double fault and out-of-bounds shots.
